The Best Movies According to Morningstar

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

Naruto

Anime with a profound and intriguing story line. I found the second-lead Sasuke Uchiha the most awe-inspiring of all the characters.

Predator

Mind-Blowing thriller cum horror movie.

The Dreamers

Awesome story of three rebellious teens, during the students protests in Paris in the 70s.
